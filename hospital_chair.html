Hospital Chair

The inspiration behind the seating design stems from having friends and family members who actively work in the medical field.

Details

The primary objective of this design is to revamp guest seating in the ICU room of a hospital. The aim is to create a seating arrangement that caters to the comfort and needs of both the patients and their visitors.

Side Profile

The designs used were inspired by Google break rooms, and revolved around creating a space that would stimulate the feeling of comfort.

Another emotion that might be invoked from this design is a calm or relaxing feeling that traditional hospital seating don't offer.

Front Quarter View

Incorporating small, intimate spaces within a larger environment, along with the integration of adaptable lighting, are thoughtful design elements that prioritize the well-being and productivity of end users.

Front Markup

By combining intimate, snug spaces with adaptable lighting choices, the environment prioritizes comfort but also empowers individuals to tailor their surroundings to match their unique tasks and emotional states.
